## 数据三层断裂 / Three Layers of Breakage

### Layer 1 — Collection / 采集
Sensors, devices, human input. Nearly every project gets this right.

### Layer 2 — Verification / 验证 ⚡
Is this data real? Was that rep actually performed? Is the person who submitted this movement data the person who actually moved?

Immutable record, tokenization, data markets. Every crypto project has this.
