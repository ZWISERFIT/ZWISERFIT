## The Category Trap

Every new protocol gets asked: *"What is this?"*

The answer usually forces it into an existing category — which is wrong by definition, because if it fit an existing category, it would already exist.

**ZWF's temptation:** Call it a "fitness Web3 project" or "DePIN for health" or "gym NFT platform."

**The truth:** Those are all labels for things that already exist. ZWF doesn't fit any of them.

---

## What ZWF Actually Is

ZWF is **the verification layer for physical behavior data.**

Think of it like TCP/IP for movement:
- TCP/IP doesn't care what data you send — it verifies the packet was delivered
- ZWF doesn't care what behavior you perform — it verifies the movement occurred

Everything else (tokenization, insurance, DePIN, RWA, community) sits on top of this layer.

---

## What This Means for Builders

If you build on ZWF, you're not building on a "fitness crypto app." You're building on a **behavior verification primitive** that can be composed into:

- Insurance protocols that reward verified movement
- DePIN networks that incentivize physical activity
- RWA markets that trade behavior-based assets
- DID systems where your workout history is your credential

**The category doesn't exist yet. We're building it.**
